Privacy Policy — Habit ring

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Habit ring ("the App") is built by Thanh Nguyen Xuan. This page informs you of the policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the App.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, the App collects:

  • Your Google account display name
  • Your Google account email address
  • Your Google account profile photo URL

All habit data (tasks, streaks, activity logs) is stored locally on your device only.

How We Use Your Information

Your Google account information is used solely to identify your account within the App. It is not shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Your Google sign-in credentials are stored securely using Android's secure storage system. No habit data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Sign-In (provided by Google LLC) for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to the sign-in process: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data by uninstalling the App. To remove Google account access, revoke App permissions in your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
